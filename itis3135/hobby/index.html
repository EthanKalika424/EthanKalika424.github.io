Antique Cars

Antique cars are very special because they could have damages, missing parts or could be all new parts making the car loose its price. There are two diffrences between antique, classic and vintage cars. The two diffrences is the age of the car and the price of the car.

Age of the car

  • Vintage Car: Manufactured between 1919 and 1930. Either a “survivor” or restored in conformance to the original manufacturer specifications
  • Antique Car: Manufactured 1975 or earlier (>45 years old). Either a “survivor” or restored in conformance to the original manufacturer specifications
  • Classic Car: Manufactured 2000 or earlier (>20 years old)

There is no criterion for determining a vintage vs. an antique car by price, But it could indicate how valuable and desiable the car can be.A collector car's sale price is determined by the make and model's rarity, desirability/demand by consumers, its condition, quality of restoration, and mileage.
